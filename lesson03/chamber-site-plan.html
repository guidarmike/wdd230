Welcome to Blue Ridge Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The Blue Ridge Chamber of Commerce was chosen as the site name to reflect the geographic location and purpose of the organization, serving businesses and organizations in the Blue Ridge region.

Site Purpose

The Blue Ridge Chamber of Commerce aims to serve as a catalyst for economic growth, community development, and networking opportunities in the region. It provides resources, advocacy, and support to businesses and organizations to thrive and contribute to the local economy.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The primary color, #0d4074, is used for headers and backgrounds to provide a strong and professional visual identity.
The accent color, #3498db, is used for buttons and accents to create contrast and emphasis.

For more information on choosing good website color schemes, visit websitebuilderexpert.com.

Typography

The heading font, Arial Black, is used for titles and headings to create a bold and attention-grabbing visual hierarchy. The body font, Helvetica Neue, is used for paragraphs and body text for readability and clarity.
